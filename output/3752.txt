[[[Biography of Submission Author]]]

[[[, Gary Sullivan OBE]]]&#xa0;

[[[Career Summary]]]&#xa0;Gary specialises in Construction Logistics, supply chain development and regeneration.  He has over 20 years of experience in the planning and operation of the logistics of complex infrastructure projects. Gary has been involved with many high profile projects with BAA, AWE, BBC, MOD and the Olympics.&#xa0;In addition to developing the UK’s foremost security and logistics contractor, from a standing start, to recognised industry leader, Gary has served in the Parachute Regiment and worked for the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees. He was commissioned into the Engineer and Logistics Staff Corps in 2011.&#xa0;Gary was awarded an OBE in Queen’s Birthday Honours List in June 2012 for Service to Regeneration of the Thames Gateway, and in October 2012 he received an Honorary Doctorate from Anglia Ruskin University in Business Administration.  He is a published author and a UNICEF Global Guardian.&#xa0;
