[[[About the author]]]

[[[Greg Power]]] has been involved in political and parliamentary reform for around 20 years. He co-founded Global Partners in 2005 to deliver projects to strengthen representative politics, since working in countries in the Middle East, sub-Saharan Africa and Latin America.  He provides direct support to politicians and ministers, developing strategies and helping to manage the process of political and parliamentary reform.  &#xa0;He has also provided advice to a variety of international organisations and donor agencies helping, amongst others, the Danish foreign ministry to establish their multi-party institute, and International IDEA to evaluate their work on political parties.  He writes widely on issues such as the application of political economy analysis, donor approaches to governance assistance, and parliamentary transparency.  He is the author of the first Global Parliamentary Report, published by the UNDP and IPU in 2012.&#xa0;
