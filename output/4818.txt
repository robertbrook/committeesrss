[[[What role can DFID play in strengthening the health system?]]]&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; To support the Ministry of Health to develop a longer term vision for the health system – and to develop innovative mechanisms and partnerships to finance it. &#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; To encourage the government to work in partnership with providers such as MSI to fill gaps, scale up services and harness potential of private providers. &#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; To encourage the government to train and build the capacity of rural health workers particularly midwives, and to empower them to provide services in isolated rural areas. &#xa0;

[[[&#xa0;]]]&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; Continue to prioritise investment in health - prioritising sexual and reproductive health services which offer the most cost effective way to improve health and development indicators. &#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; Prioritise delivery and access to primary health care services in rural areas and for excluded communities.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; Continuing to work with other donors to ensure co-ordination and pooling of resources.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; Provision of technical assistance - particularly on health financing, infrastructure investments (eg for supplies and commodities) and training health workers at scale.&#xa0;January 2013&#xa0; https://www.gov.uk/government/uploads/system/uploads/attachment_data/file/228499/ICAI-Burma-health-management-response-aug13.pdf&#xa0;
