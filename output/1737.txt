[[[Written evidence from the ]]]

[[[Tees Valley Rural Community Council]]] 

[[[(TIC 074)]]]This submission is based on TVRCC’s strategic and practical involvement in rural transport and experience of operating a community transport service in East Cleveland for the past three years.The response focusses significantly on rural residents with additional, supported travel needs – older people, people with mobility problems, and the young.The reasons for the higher costs of rural transport are discussed.There is an emphasis on working with and within rural communities, partnership working with the local authorities, and working with volunteers.The importance of some degree of subsidy to the success of transport ventures in rural areas, and innovative approaches, including the use of smaller vehicles and community car schemes, are highlighted.&#xa0;
