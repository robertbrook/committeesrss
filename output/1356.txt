[[[Xenia Dormandy, Chatham House]]]&#xa0;

[[[Xenia Dormandy]]] is the Project Director of the US Programme and the Acting Dean of the Academy for Leadership in International Affairs at Chatham House.  From 2009-2011, she was the Executive Director of the PeaceNexus Foundation in Switzerland.  Prior to this, Xenia was Executive Director of Harvard Kennedy School’s Belfer Center for Science and International Affairs, Director of the Project on India and the Subcontinent, and a member of the Board of the Belfer Center.  From 2001-05, Xenia worked at the US State Department on issues from nonproliferation, to homeland security and South Asia.  She also spent six months helping to set up the Homeland Security Office in the Office of the Vice President, and was Director for South Asia at the US National Security Council. &#xa0;
