[[[Confederation of British Industry—Written evidence]]]The CBI is the UK’s leading business organisation, speaking for some 240,000 businesses that together employ around a third of the private sector workforce.  With offices across the UK as well as representation in Brussels, Washington, Beijing and Delhi the CBI communicates the British business voice around the world. The CBI welcomes the opportunity to contribute to the inquiry into the draft Finance Bill 2014 which was published on 10 December 2013.
