[[[Summary]]]The broadcasting market has changed significantly since the BBC was first established.  With the wide range of providers now in the broadcast market a new model is required for the provision of ‘public service’ broadcasting and funding of the BBC.  The current system of a compulsory tax on all UK households to fund the BBC is both regressive and detrimental to the good management of the organization.  The BBC tax should be abolished and the BBC funded through subscription and / or advertising.  Such a system will resolve many of the governance failures evident in the current arrangements.
