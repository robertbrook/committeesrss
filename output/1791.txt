[[[When a local authority declines to impose a traffic regulation order that is widely-supported by the public, there should be a right of appeal, either to an independent inspector, or a magistrate. ]]]&#xa0;I spent 4 years on the Yorkshire Dales Access Forum and it increased my knowledge of a number of issues. Whilst YDNPA with input from YDNP LAF has now imposed permanent TRO's on the green lanes perceived, following detailed research, to be most in danger the same is not true of neighbouring authorities.&#xa0;I had long been aware of comments about the route over Deadman's Hill which being in Nidderdale falls outside the NP boundary. Pressures were being put on North Yorkshire CC to place a permanent TRO on the route. However, despite considerable public pressure, they decided to place only a temporary TRO which runs out shortly.
