[[[To what extent is local government procurement organised to deliver value for money and social, economic and environmental objectives, including stimulating the local economy? To what extent are local authorities achieving the involvement of local residents in delivering value for money? To what extent are local authorities able to develop long-term relationships with contractors?]]]&#xa0;1.1.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; Our experience of local government procurement includes a number of frameworks and individually procured contracts.  In addition to seeking the most competitive price for the completion of the construction works, the contracts often include requirements for the delivery of benefits for the local community or economy, including training, employment or economic spend commitments.&#xa0;1.2.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; The vast majority of work that we compete for requires proposals for the delivery of apprenticeships, employment opportunities and commitments to retention of spend in the local economy.  These are typically used by the client within the assessment of the bids.&#xa0;1.3.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; A prime example of a framework that has been established to deliver value for money is the North West Construction Hub (NWCH).  The NWCH delivers savings via a number of routes.&#xa0;1.4.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; The original procurement of the NWCH Framework allowed the appointment of contractors across a range of project values (low, Medium and High) based not only on their financial proposals but also on their experience and ability to deliver additional benefits, including social, economic, environmental and educational.&#xa0;1.5.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; The appointment of contractors on the framework delivers value for clients by enabling competent, committed contractors to be appointed in a shorter timescale (typically a 10-14 day period for competition).  This reduces the cost of appointment for the client.&#xa0;1.6.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; Clients are able to request specific targets and commitments from the contractors relating to their specific project.  For example, the number of apprentices that will be delivered during the works.&#xa0;1.7.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; Commitments are expected to be realistic, with contractors not only providing the proposed numbers, but also a method statement outlining how the apprentice levels will be achieved.&#xa0;1.8.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; The method statement and commitment levels provide the clients with information to regularly assess the performance of the contractor against as part of the suite of Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) that the contractor is measured against during the works.&#xa0;1.9.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; The development of the NWCH includes collaborative forums for sharing best practice to ensure all clients access the benefits of improved performance.&#xa0;1.10.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; Through the development of frameworks clients are able to develop long term relationships with contractors, as opposed to individual procurement routes.  The use of frameworks to appoint a smaller number of contractors, who can then be allocated work through call-off also aids the development of long term relationships.&#xa0;1.11.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; Early engagement of contractors does allow more time for the participation of the end user / residents in the process.  This ensures that their drivers / requirements can be incorporated into the scheme.&#xa0;1.12.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; This early engagement also benefits the assessment of the scheme in terms of sustainability and whole life costs.  The use of specialist supply chain partners can be increased if more time is allowed in the programme.&#xa0;1.13.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; The process also allows the development of local supply chain partners, as commitments to the retention of spend in the local economy can be made, and measured during the works.&#xa0;1.14.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; The development of long term partnerships, either through frameworks or from continuity of work, does allow contractors to understand, and achieve, more of their clients’ goals.&#xa0;1.15.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; Long term relationships allow contractors to invest more in education, training and employment through certainty of workload.  This includes engagement with local schools, colleges, employment agencies and subcontractors to develop, and deliver, suitable proposals.&#xa0;

[[[Do authorities take sufficient advantage of collaborative and joint procurement opportunities, including those available from central government? In addition, the Committee would welcome information on PFI contracts and their operation with local government.]]]&#xa0;2.1.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; Our experience within the North West of England is that clients have taken advantage of collaborative and joint procurement opportunities in certain areas.&#xa0;2.2.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; The development of the North West Construction Hub (NWCH) demonstrates clients accessing a joint procurement route, delivering saving on future procurement exercises by reducing the time and workload required to select a contractor.&#xa0;2.3.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; In addition, the NWCH is able to offer its procurement route to new clients within the region, allowing them to appointment contractors in reduced timescales, and access all of the monitoring and assessment systems within the framework.&#xa0;2.4.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; Other frameworks that we are members of have also provided clients with joint procurement opportunities.  This includes procurement of contractors for work, and the utilisation of joint procurement routes for materials.&#xa0;2.5.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; We have collaboratively worked with other contractors to deliver projects for clients, including on our recent framework with Lancashire County Council.  The projects allowed closer working relationships between the Council and the two contractors to be established, aiding communication, reducing costs and improving delivery of the works.&#xa0;

[[[To what extent is risk in local government procurement and contracting understood and managed and contracting strategies adopted, which are tailored to product and supplier market places? More specifically, do local authorities maintain and operate effective client management functions and have they entered contractual arrangements which allow the flexibility to meet changing circumstances such as budget reductions or changes in the way a service has to be delivered?]]]&#xa0;4.1.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; The development of partnering relationships with clients has benefited risk management, through a collaborative approach being taken.&#xa0;4.2.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; The early selection of contractor partners allows the development of accurate risk management proposals and registers, aiding project delivery.&#xa0;4.3.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; Clients have demonstrated improved willingness to engage with contractors via the frameworks, rather than a previous approach of all risk being passed to the contractor.&#xa0;4.4.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; The use of open book and target cost models allows clients to more accurately manage the budget / be informed of the costs as the project progresses.&#xa0;4.5.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; The NWCH framework does operate an effective client management function.  This includes the use of framework meetings to discuss the performance of the framework, contractors and future developments.&#xa0;4.6.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; Through the development of collaborative, partnering with our clients, both through frameworks and on traditionally tendered projects, we have delivered effective budgetary management and control.&#xa0;4.7.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; The clear identification of cost, allocation of responsibility for cost management and control, and the establishment of clear, effective lines of communication and authorisation allow costs to be managed effectively.&#xa0;4.8.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; The early involvement of contractors on projects also ensures that a clear scope of works is developed, through engagement with the end users.  For example, on the delivery of school projects that use of workshops and design festivals with the staff and pupils ensures that the works deliver what is required, not what it is thought it needed.  This removes risks from variations once work commences, protecting the project budget.&#xa0;4.9.&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0; Another benefit of the early selection and close involvement of contractors in our frameworks has been the opportunity to reduce costs on one project for reallocation to other works.  This is aided by working with clients to understand their full programme of work, only achieved if all staff work collaboratively.&#xa0;
