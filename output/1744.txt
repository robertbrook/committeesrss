[[[&#xa0;]]]The Girls’ Attitudes SurveyGirlguiding’s submission focuses on evidence from the Girls’ Attitudes Surveys, our annual research into the opinions of girls and young women throughout the UK aged 11-21, described below (points 4-6). This evidence provides unique insight into why young women’s interest in STEM subjects may drop off. Girlguiding’s youth programmes offer a good practice example of how girls’ and young women’s interest in STEM subjects can be encouraged (points 21-22).&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;Science and Engineering&#xa0;Gender bias in careers
