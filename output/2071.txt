

[[[Draft Degregulation Bill]]]City &amp; Guilds response&#xa0;&#xa0;&#xa0;September 2013 &#xa0;City &amp; Guilds is a leading vocational awarding organisation for work-based qualifications, committed to offering high quality qualifications across a wide range of industrial sectors, through 8500 colleges and training providers worldwide. We value the provision of qualifications to a high standard and support any work to ensure these standards through the strategic regulation of awarding organisations and qualifications.&#xa0;Two million people every year take City &amp; Guilds qualifications, which span levels from basic skills to the highest levels of professional achievement, and are designed to help people gain the skills employers’ value. City &amp; Guilds is also a major innovator in the field of electronic assessment (e-assessment). More than four million on-line tests have been taken by candidates entering for City &amp; Guilds qualifications.&#xa0;We welcome the opportunity to provide comments to this call for evidence and will refer specifically to areas of the Bill affecting education and training. We look forward to working with the Joint Committee on further developments.&#xa0;&#xa0;
