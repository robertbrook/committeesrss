[[[GMB Union]]] GMB is the largest trade union for school support staff, representing over 100,000 school workers nationally, an ever increasing number of whom work in academies. GMB members in schools are teaching assistants of every level, provide caretaking and school meals, and work in a range of other auxiliary and support functions.&#xa0;
