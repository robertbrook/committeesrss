[[[About EEF]]]

[[[&#xa0;]]]&#xa0;&#xa0;EEF, the manufacturers’ organisation is the representative voice of UK manufacturing, with offices in London, Brussels, every English region and Wales. We are a not for profit organisation with a growing membership of almost 6,000 companies of all sizes, employing some 900,000 people from every sector of the engineering, manufacturing and technology based industries. UK Steel, a division of EEF, is the trade association for the UK steel industry.  It represents all the country’s steelmakers and a large number of downstream steel processers.

[[[&#xa0;]]]

[[[&#xa0;]]]

[[[Response]]]

[[[&#xa0;]]]&#xa0;&#xa0;EEF welcomes the opportunity to provide our views on the Commons Select Committee’s Call for Evidence on Low Carbon Innovation.  EEF has long argued that a strong culture of innovation holds the key to overcoming technological challenges, minimising the cost of meeting future climate change targets and in capitalising on the economic opportunities from green growth.&#xa0;&#xa0;
